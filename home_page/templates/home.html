Welcome to Our News Platform!

Welcome to the ONLY website that aims to bring you nothing else than REAL and LEGITIMATE news!
If you like to stay updated and read articles from the newspapers or digital medias, we are sure that you feel like us - that is it almost impossible to avoid suffering
from fake news. If so - our system is exactly for you!

Our system lets you, as a reader, to browse and read new articles in real time, and with the help of our AI model you can decide whether the article you are reading
is fake or legitimate.
Our trained model uses advanced machine learning techniques in order to interpret the article and automatically tell you if what you are reading is legit!
In addition, if you are an expert in your field and you understand the important struggle with the legitimate of the published articles, you can join us and provide
your point of view and rate our model's performance! That way, every reader can take into consideration both of our model's predictions and your opinion!

This project's goal is to establish a system that will reduce the amount of fake news consumed by the readers.
